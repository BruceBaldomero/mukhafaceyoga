About

More than a facial —
a therapeutic experience

MUKHA is a holistic facial therapy space rooted in intentional touch, nervous system support, and natural restoration.

The name Mukha means "face" in both Tagalog — the language of the Philippines, where founder Mona is from — and in Sanskrit, the ancient language from which yoga originates. Reflecting Mona's Filipino heritage and her passion for holistic wellness, MUKHA embodies the connection between outer expression and inner wellbeing.

Blending facial myofascial release, buccal massage, face yoga, and holistic facial rituals, each treatment is designed to release tension held within the face, jaw, and body — while supporting natural sculpting, circulation, and deep relaxation.

Inspired by yoga philosophy and slow wellness practices, MUKHA offers more than a facial. It is a therapeutic experience created to help you slow down, soften, and reconnect with yourself.

Founded by Mona, a Registered Nurse and Holistic Facial Therapist, every treatment is guided by both clinical knowledge and intuitive care — creating a safe, nurturing, and restorative space for each client.

At MUKHA, beauty and wellness are viewed as deeply interconnected — where relaxation, wellbeing, and facial health exist together in harmony.

MOKSHA

Myofascial Release Massage

ANTAR

Buccal Release

15–20 min  ·  30

ANTAR translates to "inner" — representing inward awareness and release from within. This focused intra-oral technique works deeply into the cheeks and jaw through careful, precise massage to release stored tension and restore balance to the lower face.

Performed using nitrile gloves. Limited to 15–20 minutes to ensure comfort.

  • Releases deep jaw & facial tension
  • Helps relieve teeth grinding (bruxism)
  • Slims and sculpts the lower face
  • Supports facial symmetry & relaxation
