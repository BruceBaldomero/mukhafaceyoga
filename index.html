About

More than a facial —
a therapeutic experience

MUKHA is a holistic facial therapy space rooted in intentional touch, nervous system care and natural restoration.

Blending facial myofascial release, buccal massage, face yoga and holistic facial rituals, each treatment is designed to release tension held within the face, jaw and body — while supporting natural sculpting, circulation and deep relaxation.

Inspired by yoga philosophy and slow wellness practices, MUKHA offers more than a facial. It is a therapeutic experience created to help you pause, soften and reconnect with yourself.

Founded by Mona, a Registered Nurse and Holistic Facial Therapist, treatments are approached with both clinical understanding and intuitive care — creating a safe and restorative space for each client.

At MUKHA, beauty and wellness is approached holistically — where relaxation, wellbeing and facial health exist together in balance.

ANTAR

Buccal Release

15–20 min  ·  30

ANTAR translates to "inner" — representing inward awareness and release from within. This focused intra-oral technique works deeply into the cheeks and jaw through careful, precise massage to release stored tension and restore balance to the lower face.
